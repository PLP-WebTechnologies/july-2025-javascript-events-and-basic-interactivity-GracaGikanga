FAQ

What is the best way to start eating healthier?
Begin by adding more whole foods like fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Reduce processed foods and sugary drinks gradually instead of cutting everything at once.
How much water should I drink every day?
About 8 glasses (2 liters) per day, but needs vary depending on activity level, climate, and individual health. Listen to your body and hydrate regularly.
Can a healthy diet help with weight loss?
Yes! A balanced diet combined with regular physical activity is a sustainable way to lose weight. Focus on nutrient-dense foods and portion control. Consulting a registered dietitian can help tailor a plan to your needs.
Is it okay to skip meals if I had a heavy breakfast?
Not recommended. Skipping meals can lead to energy crashes and poor digestion. Instead, spread your calorie intake evenly throughout the day.
